🚀 Analyst Lab Africa Internship – Week 5 | Telco Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

Excited to share my Week 5 project during my Data Analytics Internship at Analyst Lab Africa, where I analyzed the Telco Customer Churn Dataset and built an interactive dashboard in Power BI to understand customer behavior and identify the key drivers of churn.

📌 Project Objective

The primary goal of this project was to answer two critical business questions:

Why are customers leaving the company?
Which factors contribute most to customer churn?

By analyzing customer demographics, service subscriptions, billing information, and account details, I identified the major factors influencing customer retention and provided actionable business recommendations.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Microsoft Excel
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
📊 Dataset Overview

The dataset consists of 7,043 customer records and 21 variables, including:

Contract Type
Monthly Charges
Tenure
Internet Service
Payment Method
Customer Demographics
Customer Churn Status
📈 Dashboard Features

The interactive dashboard includes:

✅ Customer Churn Distribution
✅ Contract Type vs Churn Analysis
✅ Monthly Charges by Churn
✅ Tenure Analysis
✅ Internet Service vs Churn
✅ Payment Method vs Churn
✅ KPI Cards (Total Customers, Churned Customers, Churn Rate)

🔍 Key Findings

The analysis revealed several important insights:

Customers on month-to-month contracts are more likely to churn than those on long-term contracts.
Customers with higher monthly charges tend to leave the company more frequently.
New customers with shorter tenure represent the highest-risk group for churn.
Fiber optic users exhibit higher churn rates compared to DSL customers.
Customers using electronic checks are more likely to churn than those using automatic payment methods.
💡 Business Insights

The findings indicate that customer commitment, pricing, service quality, and payment convenience significantly impact customer retention. Businesses can reduce churn by focusing on high-risk customer segments and improving the overall customer experience.

✅ Recommendations

Based on the analysis, I proposed the following recommendations:

Promote long-term contracts through discounts and incentives.
Strengthen onboarding and engagement programs for new customers.
Introduce loyalty programs for high-value customers.
Improve customer satisfaction among fiber optic users.
Encourage customers to adopt automatic payment methods.
🎯 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can transform raw customer data into actionable business insights. By identifying the key drivers of customer churn, organizations can implement effective retention strategies, improve customer satisfaction, and increase long-term profitability.
